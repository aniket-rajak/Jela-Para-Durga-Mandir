Our Programme

Join our spiritual activities and events

Regular Programmes

We offer a variety of spiritual programmes throughout the year for devotees of all ages.

Daily 8:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Morning & Evening Puja

Daily prayers conducted at sunrise and sunset. Devotees can participate in the sacred rituals and receive prasad.

Saturdays 4:00 PM

Bhajan Sandhya

Evening devotional music session where devotees gather to sing traditional hymns and kirtans in praise of Goddess Durga.

Sundays 10:00 AM

Weekly Satsang

Spiritual discourse and discussion on ancient scriptures, followed by aarti and prasad distribution.

Monthly Amavasya

Amavasya Tarpan

Special rituals performed on new moon days for ancestral worship and spiritual benefits.

Special Programmes

October 5 Days

Durga Puja

The biggest festival of our temple! Five days of elaborate pujas, cultural programs, and festive celebrations. Thousands of devotees join to seek the blessings of Goddess Durga.

November 1 Day

Kali Puja

Special puja dedicated to Goddess Kali. The temple is decorated with lights and flowers for this powerful ritual.

January/Feb 1 Day

Saraswati Puja

Worship of Goddess Saraswati for wisdom and knowledge. Students and educators particularly benefit from this special puja.

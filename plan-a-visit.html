Welcome to Jela Para Durga Mandir

We warmly welcome all devotees and visitors to our sacred temple. Plan your visit to experience the divine blessings of Goddess Durga.

What to Expect

  • Daily morning and evening prayers
  • Special puja on Navratri
  • Opportunity for darshan of the deity
  • Prasad distribution after puja
  • Peaceful environment for meditation

Guidelines for Visitors

  • Remove shoes before entering the temple premises
  • Maintain silence and decorum
  • Dress modestly and appropriately
  • Photography is not allowed inside the sanctum
  • Mobile phones should be on silent mode

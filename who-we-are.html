Who We Are

A community dedicated to spiritual growth

Our Story

Jela Para Durga Mandir has been a beacon of spiritual light in Purulia, West Bengal for generations. Our temple is dedicated to Goddess Durga, the divine mother who symbolizes strength, courage, and protection.

Our History

Established over 100 years ago, our temple has been a center of religious activities and community gatherings for the people of Purulia and surrounding areas. The temple architecture reflects the traditional Bengali style, with intricate carvings and a peaceful ambiance that attracts devotees from far and wide.

Our Mission

  • To preserve and promote ancient Hindu traditions and rituals
  • To provide a sacred space for devotees to connect with the divine
  • To serve the community through charitable activities
  • To educate younger generations about our cultural heritage
  • To maintain spiritual harmony and peace in our community

Our Community

We are a vibrant community of devotees, volunteers, and well-wishers who come together to celebrate our faith, perform rituals, and support each other. Our community spans across all age groups and backgrounds, united in our devotion to Goddess Durga.

Our Values

  • Devotion: Wholehearted dedication to spiritual practice
  • Service: Serving others with compassion and humility
  • Tradition: Preserving our cultural heritage
  • Unity: Bringing people together in faith
  • Integrity: Maintaining transparency and honesty
